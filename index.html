Retirement wealth specialists

We are a client-focused financial firm providing comprehensive Wealth Management Services - a team of professionals that provide personalized portfolios, host regular review meetings, and put care into everything we do.

Established in 1998 by founders Mitchell B. Walk and Bruce S. Udell, Retirement Wealth Specialists has long been committed to providing comprehensive wealth management services. We now have a team of professionals with over 122 years of combined experience in the financial services industry. Our experience over the years has taught us the importance of asking targeted questions that go beyond basic financial planning. We incorporate investment management, tax reduction strategies and family wealth planning into your overall wealth management plan.

As an independent firm, we have access to a vast array of resources, as well as the freedom to develop individualized solutions that work for you. You are much more than a series of numbers. You are a unique individual with personal goals and priorities. We skip generic models and create truly personalized portfolios. - Wealth Plans Designed for You.

We Create
Your Personalized Portfolio

The creation of your wealth management plan begins with you - your priorities, and your goals. Here at Retirement Wealth Specialists, our advisors combine an understanding of your financial objectives with our vast investment selection to create your personalized portfolio.

We Review
Your Progress Regularly

As economic conditions and your personal situations change, we believe it is important to regularly review your investment strategy. In your review meetings, our advisors have the opportunity to assess your portfolio performance, update your financial objectives and reallocate your portfolio as needed.
