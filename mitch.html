Your RWS Team

Mitchell B. Walk, AAMS®

President & Senior Advisor

Mitch is Co-founder and President of Retirement Wealth Specialists, a wealth services firm. He specializes in creating personalized portfolios by understanding clients’ unique needs and goals. His extensive experience in investment management and alternative investments allows him to recommend relevant investments for each portfolio.

As a result of his success, Mitch has been quoted in Investment News Magazine, featured in NFP Advisor Focus Magazine and has participated in forums for various organizations throughout the United States.

Since graduating with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Psychology from Flagler College in Saint Augustine, Florida, he has continued to be a leader in the Flagler community. Mitch is a member of the prestigious Board of Trustees where he is Chairman of the Institutional Advancement Committee and sits on both the Facilities Committee and Enrollment Committee. The college awarded Mitch the inaugural Flagler College Service Award, presented to alumni who have rendered a high degree of service to the school for more than 10 years. Mitch has also been honored by the college as a member of the Omicron Delta Kappa, The National Leadership Honor Society.

Mitch received his Accredited Asset Management Specialist® certification from the College for Financial Planning®.

He holds a Series 7: General Securities Representative License, Series 63: Uniform Securities Agent State Law Examination License and Series 65: Uniform Investment Adviser Law Examination License.
